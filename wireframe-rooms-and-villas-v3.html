Rooms & Villas

Designed around your well-being, morning to night

Rooms and villas are thoughtfully designed to support your well-being from morning to night. Generous dressing and bathing areas create a sense of ease, while king-size beds with natural fibre mattresses, pillow choices, and blackout curtains help encourage deeper rest. Fewer distractions, a nourishing wellness mini-bar, and in every villa, a private pool makes each stay feel calm, considered, and beautifully restorative.

Open, light-filled spaces create a feeling of simplicity and serenity, while indigenous materials and exceptional local craftsmanship ground each villa and room in its setting. Outdoor pools and terraces invite movement, conversation, contemplation, and quiet reflection. Each captures the lyrical beauty of the surrounding coast and landscape, telling a distinctive Red Sea story with elegance and restraint.

Architecture by the renowned Heah and Co. Interior design by Studio Carter.

Every way of staying

A room for every guest

From one-bedroom terrace rooms to over-water villas and one, two, three, four, and five-bedroom configurations, there is a room for every guest and every way of staying. Each villa carries a private terrace, lounge chairs, and a private pool. Many include both indoor and outdoor shower options. COCO-MAT™ natural mattresses and bespoke oud-inspired amenities are throughout. Every room includes a Jayasom flask and Wellness journal.

JAYELA · Accessibility

Accessible Rooms

Within the Jayela Terraces are rooms specifically designed for guests who prefer not to have steps. Families with young children, or those who appreciate assistance with mobility, will find a range of conveniences: electrically assisted beds, vibrating and lighted fire alarms, an emergency call system, easy-access showers and toilets, grab rails, and adjusted heights for hand basins and baths.

Jayela Terrace rooms are directly accessible by elevator to the Jayela reception, the Loumi Rue restaurant, the Jayela pool, and the play spaces.

Can I bring a pet?

Unfortunately we cannot accommodate pets in the resort.

Can I bring a nanny?

Yes. Carers and nannies can be added as part of your booking.

How many children can I fit in a room? Can I have extra beds?

Answer to be confirmed.

What is the minimum age for JAYANA?

Guests must be 16 or above.

Are there any age restrictions for JAYELA?

None — JAYELA welcomes guests of all ages.

Tell me more about multi-bedroom villas.

Multi-bedroom villas comprise a lead villa (with arrival pavilion, lounge, powder room, dining, and pantry) and one or more additional standalone villa structures. These are separate buildings, connected by private paths and landscaping.
