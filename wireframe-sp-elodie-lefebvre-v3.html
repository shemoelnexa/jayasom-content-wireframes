About

Originally from France, Elodie Lefebvre has spent fifteen years shaping wellness experiences at internationally recognised destinations. Her family practised alternative healing; that grounding deepened through her own recovery, where yoga and meditation became central. What began as personal healing became a lifelong commitment to creating spaces where others could find their way back.

Leadership roles across Asia and the Indian Ocean sit behind her; teams of more than one hundred and twenty people have moved with her, alongside pre-opening projects and the mentoring of Wellness Directors across international properties. Empathy, respect, and collaboration are the registers she leads in.

At Jayasom Wellness Resort AMAALA, Elodie leads health and wellness strategy, from concept and facility design through to medical and experiential programming. Time in nature and a sensitivity to people and place sit beneath an intuitive style, she reads what is often unspoken, so each guest's stay feels personal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who leads Health and Wellness at Jayasom AMAALA?

Elodie Lefebvre is the Director of Health and Wellness, Jayasom Wellness Resort AMAALA.

What is Elodie's training?

She trained in yoga, meditation, Reiki, Craniosacral therapy, Chi Nei Tsang, plant-based nutrition, and wellness counselling, alongside formal education in business and human resources.
