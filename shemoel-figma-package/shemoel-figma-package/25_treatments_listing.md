## Module 10: FAQ — AEO
*Flag: NEW: module — SEO ADD*

### H3
Frequently asked questions

**Q1:**
How many treatments are available at Jayasom AMAALA?

**A1:**
Sixty treatments are published. The full operational catalogue runs to more than one hundred and fifty; the Wellness Advisor draws on the wider set when a guest's plan needs it.

**Q2:**
What are the Therapeutic Pillars?

**A2:**
Three groupings: Body work, Health work, and Aesthetic. Most retreat weeks draw from all three; the Wellness Advisor decides the weighting.

**Q3:**
Can I book treatments without a retreat?

**A3:**
Day-guest treatments are available subject to capacity. The booking team confirms availability per date; the Wellness Advisor consultation is included.

**Q4:**
Do treatments include consultations?

**A4:**
A Wellness Advisor consultation is included with any retreat booking and with most stand-alone treatment bookings of more than ninety minutes.
