## Module 10: FAQ — AEO
*Flag: NEW: module — SEO ADD*

**Q1:**
What group activities are available at Jayasom AMAALA?

**A1:**
Twenty-two adult classes and thirty-five children's activities across the week, grouped into yoga, breath, dance, sound, aqua, meditation, strength, stretch, Pilates, and Tai Chi. The exact schedule shifts; we confirm it daily.

**Q2:**
Do I need to book the classes?

**A2:**
No. Group activities are open to all guests in residence; no booking, no extra fee. Capacity is generous; if a session is full at the door, the next one is on the same week's calendar.

**Q3:**
Can children join adult classes?

**A3:**
The Family slot in the late afternoon is designed for parents and children together. Outside that, adults practise in adult sessions and children practise in age-banded sessions. Some Visiting Practitioners run cross-age workshops; these are flagged on the calendar.

**Q4:**
Are sessions adapted for beginners?

**A4:**
Most sessions take all levels. The instructor sets the day's intensity from who turns up. Tell them at the start if you're new to a practice; they'll cue accordingly.
