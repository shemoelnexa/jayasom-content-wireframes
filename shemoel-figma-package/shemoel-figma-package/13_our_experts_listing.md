## Module 19: FAQ — AEO
*Flag: NEW: module — SEO ADD*

**Q1:**
What is a Wellness Advisor at Jayasom?

**A1:**
A Wellness Advisor is the resident practitioner who builds and holds your retreat plan across the stay. They consult before arrival, refine the plan in person, and adjust through the week.

**Q2:**
Who are the Visiting Practitioners?

**A2:**
Specialists who join the team in residence for set windows. They run treatments and sessions that aren't on the year-round calendar. The directory of upcoming residencies is published on this page.

**Q3:**
Can I request a specific practitioner?

**A3:**
Speak to the Wellness Advisor in your pre-arrival consultation. Where a practitioner is in residence and the schedule allows, the request is honoured. Where it isn't, the Wellness Advisor will propose the closest fit.
