## Module 3: Description
*Flag: NEW: module*

### H2
What the ritual is

**Body, paragraph 1 (what it is):**
A two-hour ritual in three layers. The Grounding Ritual carries the opening, with weighted pressure, earthy botanicals, and a slow-handed cadence. The BIOME Facial follows, anchored in microbiome-restoration practice. The closing layer is shared rather than separate: the room stays quiet for both, and the session ends without rush.

**Body, paragraph 2 (why):**
What couples find in QALBAIN is the kind of attention that does not require talking. The work is small and constant; it carries across a stay rather than holding inside a single session. The ritual is usually scheduled late in the week, once the body has settled into the wider programme.

**Body, paragraph 3 (how it feels):**
Warm, slow, shared. Two practitioners work in parallel, and breath usually settles into a shared pace within the first twenty minutes. Most couples notice it without being prompted.

How the ritual works
