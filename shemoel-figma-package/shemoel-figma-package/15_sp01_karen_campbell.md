Karen Campbell has long believed that wellbeing is not a luxury, but a birthright. Raised in New Zealand in a family where healthy living was instinctive and intuitive, and growing fruits and vegetables, tending to sheep and bees, preserving and conserving, and recycling was part of daily life.
