## Module 3: Description
*Flag: NEW: module*

### H2
What the Jayasom Signature Massage is

**Body, paragraph 1 (what it is):**
The signature blends three lineages of bodywork into one continuous session. Bamboo and walnut exfoliation lifts the surface; Lomilomi flowing strokes work the long lines; Shiatsu acupressure addresses meridian points; Thai oil pressure releases held tension along the limbs. The four pass through one practitioner, one room, one rhythm.

**Body, paragraph 2 (why):**
The session was built as the property's anchor treatment because no single tradition addresses the full body. Layering them shortens the time it takes for a guest to settle, and gives the practitioner more places to read what the body is asking for. The work is small and constant, the kind that holds up across a year.

**Body, paragraph 3 (how it feels):**
The room is warm; the oil is warmed before it touches skin. The exfoliation is brisk, the bodywork that follows is unhurried. Most guests notice the breath slowing in the first ten minutes. The practitioner works with what arrives rather than against it.

How the session works

**Body:**
Therapeutic Pillars: body work primarily, with mind-side downstream effects on sleep and stress regulation. Lineages drawn on within one session: Hawaiian Lomilomi, Japanese Shiatsu, Thai oil massage. Bamboo and walnut shells are sourced for the exfoliation rather than synthetic exfoliants.
