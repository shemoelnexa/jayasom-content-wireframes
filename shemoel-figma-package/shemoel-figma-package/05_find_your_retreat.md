Q: What is AMAALA? A: AMAALA is a wellness destination on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast, at Triple Bay. Jayasom AMAALA is one of twenty-four Phase 1 communities within the master plan. The destination is reached via Al Wajh Airport (one hour by car) or Red Sea International Airport (under two hours).
