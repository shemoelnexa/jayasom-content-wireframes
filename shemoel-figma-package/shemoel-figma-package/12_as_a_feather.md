Detox and Weight Management Retreat at Jayasom AMAALA: Featured Treatments

**Intro paragraph:**
Six detox-paired treatments hold the As a Feather week. Two of them are recurring rather than one-off: the daily one-to-one with your Wellness Advisor, and the Nutritional Consultation, which calibrates the nourishment approach across the week.

**Card 1: Detox Body Wrap:**
Detox Body Wrap | Duration: 90 minutes | A full-body wrap that draws on mineral-rich clays and herbal preparations to support the skin's clearance pathways. The body is warmed slowly; the wrap holds the heat long enough for the lymphatic system to settle into a cleansing rhythm. Available across all stay tiers.

**Card 2: Detoxifying Balneotherapy:**
Detoxifying Balneotherapy | Duration: 60 minutes | A mineral-water immersion designed to support fluid clearance and ease metabolic load. The water is warmed and lightly carbonated; the body sits supported and still. Available from seven nights upward.

**Card 3: Far Infrared Therapy:**
Far Infrared Therapy | Duration: 45 minutes | Heat-based detox in a low-radiant infrared cabin. The session reaches deep tissue without strain on the cardiovascular system, suitable for sedentary-adjacent guests for whom higher-intensity sweating is not yet appropriate. Available across all stay tiers.

**Card 4: Taoist Abdominal Massage:**
Taoist Abdominal Massage (Chi Nei Tsang) | Duration: 75 minutes | A lineage-specific abdominal practice from the Taoist tradition. Slow pressure works through the visceral organs, supporting digestive function, fluid clearance, and the gut-brain axis. Available from seven nights upward.

**Card 5: Therapeutic Lymphatic Drainage:**
Therapeutic Lymphatic Drainage | Duration: 60 minutes | A light-pressure manual technique that follows the body's lymphatic pathways, easing fluid load and supporting the immune system. Particularly relevant for guests arriving with sluggish digestion or chronic puffiness. Available across all stay tiers.

**Card 6: Udvarthanam:**
Udvarthanam (Ayurvedic Herbal Powder Massage) | Duration: 60 minutes | A herbal-powder body massage from the Ayurvedic tradition. Worked against the natural growth of body hair, the powders promote circulation and lymphatic drainage, and the practice has long been associated with weight management and skin clarity. Available from seven nights upward.

### H2
Your Days at Feather

**Paragraph 1: arrival shape:**
Each day takes its shape on the morning you arrive. Your Wellness Advisor sits down with you, reads the questionnaire you completed in the weeks before, and listens to where the body is on the day. The programme is built from there. The conversation runs longer than a standard arrival intake; what you say on the day shapes the week.

**Paragraph 2: typical day:**
A typical As a Feather day begins gently. Pranayama, Tai Chi, or Yin Yoga run at seven; you choose by mood and by what the body has settled into overnight. The eleven o'clock Stretch is the universal anchor across every retreat at Jayasom and lands here too. Lunch follows by one, lighter than dinner, drawn from the Gut Healing and Cleansing menu calibrated to the work of the stay. The afternoon holds one of the restorative detox treatments, Detox Body Wrap, Far Infrared, Detoxifying Balneotherapy, or Therapeutic Lymphatic Drainage, and recovery sits inside the day rather than added on top of it. A walk along the coastline or up to the Stonecrop Maze frames the early evening, and dinner sits at half past seven. By nine, the body is asking for the bed, and the room is set up to oblige.

**Paragraph 3: room + pacing:**
Three meals each day from the kitchen, beginning with dinner on arrival. The room is set up around your stated preferences: aroma options of Calming Lavender or Spirited Rosemary, a chosen pillow from the seven on the menu, a Coco-mat™ natural mattress. Pacing is part of the practice. Each day stays open enough to catch what the body and the cleansing rhythm surface.

The Setting at AMAALA

**Paragraph 1: property opener:**
The buildings step back where the coast asks them to and stay low against the line of the sea. Sea breeze handles the cooling load before any mechanical system gets involved. Materials are local wherever the climate allows.

For As a Feather guests, the relevant zone is Jayana, the adults retreat area at the southern end of Triple Bay. Two Jayana-side gardens sit closest to the lightness-and-clearance register of the retreat.

**Yasmeen Night Garden:**
The Yasmeen Night Garden takes its name from the jasmine that opens only after dark. Its fragrance lands lightly on the path at dusk; the air softens around it, suited to the slower pacing of a detox week. The garden sits at the foot of the Wellness Plateau and is reached through a moon gate, where reclining loungers angle towards the night sky and the path of the moon.

**Stonecrop Maze:**
The Stonecrop Maze sits on the plateau above. The maze, sometimes referred to in body text as a labyrinth for what it offers in function, invites slow walking with attention on breath and on each step the foot makes; the centre marks a moment of reflection. Stonecrop is a succulent that grows on rocks and stores water; the plant associates with peace, perseverance, and calmness. For Feather guests, the slow-walking pace of the maze pairs naturally with the quieter intensity of the cleansing rhythm.

**Phoenix + Family-side gardens:**
Phoenix Garden sits near Saratô on the Jayana side and is open to all guests. Family-side gardens (the Colour Garden chakra journey and the Poa Garden Theatre amphitheatre) sit on the Jayela side and are also accessible to any guest.
