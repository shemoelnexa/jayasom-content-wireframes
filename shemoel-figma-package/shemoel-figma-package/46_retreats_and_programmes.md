## Module 10: Tier ladder — durations
*Flag: NEW: module*

### H2
The four lengths

**Body:**
Each retreat is offered at four lengths. Four nights to get the rhythm. Seven nights for a full programme cycle. Ten nights for a deeper second half. Fourteen nights for the work that compounds. The tier you choose shapes treatment counts and the depth of the plan; the structure stays the same.

**Tier ladder card grid:**
Explore (4 nights) · Absorb (7 nights) · Immerse (10 nights) · Commit (14 nights)

## Module 16: FAQ — AEO
*Flag: NEW: module — SEO ADD*

**Q1:**
How long are the retreats at Jayasom?

**A1:**
Each retreat is offered at four, seven, ten, or fourteen nights. The shorter Taste of Jayasom is a separate three-night introduction, not a retreat sub-tier.

**Q2:**
What are the six signature retreats?

**A2:**
As a Pair, As a Woman, As a Family, As a Leader, As a Flow, and As a Feather. Each is built for a different starting point; all draw from the same Therapeutic Pillars and the same operational team.

**Q3:**
Can I do a retreat at any time of year?

**A3:**
Yes. Retreats run year-round and start on any day; we don't operate fixed start days. The Wellness Advisor will set the rhythm against your arrival.

**Q4:**
Do retreats include treatments and consultations?

**A4:**
Yes. The standard inclusions cover the Wellness Advisor consultation, a tier-appropriate count of treatments, daily group activities, and breakfast through dinner across the stay.
