**Body paragraph (architecture provenance):**
Architecture is by Heah and Co. Interiors are by Studio Carter. Materials draw on the local palette where the climate allows; the layout sits low against the coastline so the place itself does the loudest work. Each residence carries a private plunge pool angled to the Red Sea horizon.

**Body paragraph (interior detail):**
Studio Carter's interior detailing carries a pause-object register: smaller objects placed where the eye and hand naturally rest, materials warmed to the touch, and natural light shaped through the day. Coco-mat natural mattresses run across every bedroom. The kitchen carries a fitted layout calibrated to the residence's day-rhythm.

**Body paragraph (Owner Benefits):**
Owners receive the Jayasom Longevity Assessment Programme, a seven-component annual baseline carried out across the Health and Wellness Centre. Cardiovascular, metabolic, hormonal, neurocognitive, gut, sleep and movement markers run on a calibrated cadence. Standing access to the resort's amenity map sits alongside.

**Body paragraph (Wellness Advisor continuity):**
The same Wellness Advisor who shapes a retreat stay shapes the residence cycle. Pre-arrival conversations, in-residence consultations and post-departure follow-up sit within one continuous file. Owners arrive into a known relationship; the body of work built across earlier retreats threads into the longer cadence.

**Body paragraph (brand positioning):**
Jayasom is a wellness destination, not a luxury hospitality concept. The work is precise, measurable and answerable to the body in front of us. Place is a method, not a backdrop. Residences hold this register as much as the resort villas do; the practice runs across the property without distinction.

An optional rental programme runs through the resort operator. Owners place residences into the rental pool when not in residence; operator-managed turnover, housekeeping and guest experience hold the standard. Direct enquiries sit with the residences team. {TBC}
