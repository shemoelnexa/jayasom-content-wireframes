## Module 5: Method
*Flag: NEW: module*

### H2
How the session works

**Body:**
The session draws on Body across the Therapeutic Pillars and pairs Hydrafacial's clinical method with a manual lymphatic-drainage opening. The protocol runs through six steps: lymphatic drainage along the face and neck, cleanse, chemical peel, painless extraction with the Hydrafacial wand, antioxidant and peptide infusion, and a closing red and blue LED phase. The practitioner reads the skin between steps and adapts solution intensity to what the face presents. The session pairs naturally with the Digital Skin Scan consultation, which sits ahead of skin work for guests staying longer than three nights.
