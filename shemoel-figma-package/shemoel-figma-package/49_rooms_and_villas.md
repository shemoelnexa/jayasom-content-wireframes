## Module 25: Rooms density backfill (deep)
*Flag: NEW: append element*

**Body paragraph (villa types canonical):**
Sixty villas hold the resort across four core layouts: One-Bedroom Beachfront Pool Villa at sixty-two square metres indoor and forty-eight square metres outdoor; Two-Bedroom Beachfront Pool Villa for guests travelling together; Three-Bedroom Pool Villa for cohort or family stays; and Over-Water Villas facing the bay for guests on longer stays. Each layout carries a balcony, lounge chairs, plunge pool, Coco-mat natural mattress, and Oud-custom amenities.

**Body paragraph (room set-up canonical):**
Each room is set up around stated preferences before arrival. Aroma options: Calming Lavender or Spirited Rosemary. Pillow choice from the seven on the menu. The room held to the property's temperature standard. Coco-mat natural mattress: latex, coconut fibre, horse hair, cactus fibre, seaweed, knitted cotton.

**Body paragraph (Jayana versus Jayela):**
Two zones hold the property. Jayana sits at the southern end and runs the adult-side retreat work, with Yasmeen Night Garden, Stonecrop Maze, Phoenix Garden, Saratô, Jiraka, and the adult Health and Wellness Centre. Jayela sits at the northern end and runs the family-side work, with the Growing Studios, Loumi Rue, Chez Mitzuba, Breezy Kale, and the family pools.

**Body paragraph (residences alongside):**
Twenty-four wellness residences sit alongside the resort villas across three layouts: ten two-bedroom at three hundred and fifty square metres, twelve three-bedroom at four hundred and twenty-three square metres, and two four-bedroom at five hundred and twenty-five square metres. Architecture is by Heah and Co; interiors by Studio Carter. A five-bedroom Jayela Residence with private wellness studio sits as a separate flagship layout.

**Body paragraph (architecture and interiors):**
Heah and Co designed the architecture for both villas and residences. Studio Carter shaped the interiors. Materials draw on the local palette where the climate allows; buildings recede in the right places and sit low against the coastline; sea breeze moves before mechanical cooling.

**Body paragraph (in-room amenities):**
Each room carries a private balcony, lounge chairs, and a private plunge pool. The Jayasom Collection welcome amenity sits in every room on arrival. Wellness minibar contents are stocked according to the Specialist Consultation; the Coco-mat natural mattress runs across every layout; Oud-custom amenities sit alongside.

**Body paragraph (place-as-method):**
Place is a method, not a backdrop. The villa layouts hold the practice as much as the studios do. Sixty villas plus twenty-four residences hold the property: phase one is a small property by destination-resort scale, and the design is for it to stay that way. The daily wellness rhythm is sized to the cohort, not to the building.
