## Module 18: View Insight template density backfill (deep)
*Flag: NEW: append element*

**Body paragraph (article template framing):**
Insights from the Jayasom team carry a single piece of editorial work across the page. Each piece is written by a named author and reviewed by a clinical or brand reviewer. Reading time sits at the top of the page; published date and last-updated date sit alongside. The work threads back to the wider Insights category.

**Body paragraph (reading-time signal):**
Reading time sits as a signal at the top of every Insight piece, calibrated against the average reading speed for the genre. Short reflections at three to five minutes; long-form Journal pieces at eight to fifteen minutes; Thought Leadership pieces at fifteen to twenty-five minutes. Each carries its own pacing.

**Body paragraph (author bio block):**
Each Insight piece carries a brief author bio at the foot of the page: the practitioner's name, role at Jayasom, and a one-line credential anchor. Where the author is a Visiting Practitioner, the bio names the residency and links across to their wider profile in the Specialists directory.

**Body paragraph (Reviewed-by canonical):**
A clinical or brand reviewer sits alongside the named author on every Insight piece. The Reviewed-by line names the reviewer's role and links to their full Specialist profile. Clinical pieces are reviewed by a member of the Health and Wellness Centre; brand and editorial pieces are reviewed by the Director of Marketing, PR and Communications.

**Body paragraph (related articles):**
Related articles sit at the foot of every Insight piece, drawing from the same Therapeutic Pillar or the same retreat shape. Internal cross-links thread across the editorial programme; external citations sit within the body where a study or canonical source is referenced. The piece holds within the wider Insights cluster.

**Body paragraph (subscribe and share):**
A subscribe block sits at the foot of every Insight piece. Stay close to Jayasom: receive seasonal reflections from the team, news from the wellness destination, and editorial pieces from the practitioners who anchor the work. Share buttons sit alongside for readers who want to send the piece on.
