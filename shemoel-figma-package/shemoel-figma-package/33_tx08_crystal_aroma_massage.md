What the session is

**Body, paragraph 1 (what it is):**
A bodywork session in three layers: aromatherapy, heated stone, energy work. The Jade is held at body temperature on entry and warmed by the room as the session proceeds; the oil blend is chosen at the start by the practitioner from a small palette of Arabic-inspired essences.

**Body, paragraph 2 (why):**
Across a stay, the work is small and constant. Muscle release without deep pressure draws most guests; some come for the heat that lengthens the breath, others for the quiet that scent and weighted touch leave behind.

Warm, slow, scented. Jade carries weight in the hand; strokes run long. Most guests notice the room before the practitioner begins, and the breath before the muscle release.

## Module 9: FAQ
*Flag: NEW: module — SEO ADD*

### H2
Common questions

**FAQ Q+A 1:**
Q: Why Jade rather than another stone? A: The crystal carries weight the hand alone cannot match, and it holds heat well. Other stones in the property's range include heated salt and basalt; the practitioner chooses with the brief.

**FAQ Q+A 2:**
Q: Are the oils Arabic? A: Arabic-inspired. The blend draws on the Traditional Arabic Medicine cluster the property has built around: oud, frankincense, jasmine, rose, neroli among them. The blend is matched to the brief, not to a fixed protocol.

**FAQ Q+A 3:**
Q: Is this the same as Gemstone Therapy or Crystal Healing? A: They sit alongside each other in the property's catalogue but they are distinct. Crystal Aroma Massage is a bodywork session with heated Jade and aromatherapy. Gemstone Therapy and Crystal Healing each have their own structure.

**FAQ Q+A 4:**
Q: Will the pressure be deep? A: Light to medium. The Jade delivers the weight; the hand directs it. Guests who want deeper pressure usually move to Deep Tissue or Pinda Sweda within the same week.

**FAQ Q+A 5:**
Q: What does the Energy Work Pillar mean here? A: Energy and Sound is one of the Therapeutic Pillars at Jayasom. The pillar covers Reiki, Gemstone Therapy, Sound Healing, and Crystal Aroma Massage among others; the work is felt rather than discussed.
