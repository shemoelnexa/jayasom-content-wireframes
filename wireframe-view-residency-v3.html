A note on the practice

Place is part of the practice

Studio Carter's interior detailing carries a pause-object register: smaller objects placed where the eye and hand naturally rest, materials warmed to the touch, and natural light shaped through the day. Coco-mat natural mattresses run across every bedroom. The kitchen carries a fitted layout calibrated to the residence's day-rhythm.

Owners receive the Jayasom Longevity Assessment Programme, a seven-component annual baseline carried out across the Health and Wellness Centre. Cardiovascular, metabolic, hormonal, neurocognitive, gut, sleep and movement markers run on a calibrated cadence. Standing access to the resort's amenity map sits alongside.

Jayasom is a wellness destination, not a luxury hospitality concept. The work is precise, measurable and answerable to the body in front of us. Place is a method, not a backdrop. Residences hold this register as much as the resort villas do; the practice runs across the property without distinction.
