Hydrafacial Platinum

The full Hydrafacial protocol with lymphatic drainage.

Overview

What the session is

What it is

The longest of three

The Platinum tier is the longest of three Hydrafacial options at Jayasom, after the thirty-minute Signature and the forty-five-minute Deluxe. The added length sits with the lymphatic-drainage step at the opening, which works along the face and neck before the cleanse begins. The full protocol then runs through Hydrafacial's six-step sequence to the antioxidant close.

Why guests choose it

A skin reading that has been due

Guests come to the Platinum for a skin reading that has been due for a while: travel-load showing in the face, dull tone, congestion that has not cleared with home routine. The work is small and constant, the kind that holds up across a year. The change registers as skin that reads brighter, congestion that has cleared along the jawline and forehead, and a face that holds hydration through the day.

How it feels

Quiet, immediate

A quiet room, a slow opening across face and neck under the practitioner's hands, then the Hydrafacial wand running through cleanse, exfoliate and extract steps, and a closing infusion that warms the skin under LED. No downtime; the face reads brighter immediately, more even within the day.

Method

The six-step protocol

The session draws on Body across the Therapeutic Pillars and pairs Hydrafacial's clinical method with a manual lymphatic-drainage opening. The practitioner reads the skin between steps and adapts solution intensity to what the face presents.

Step 01

Lymphatic drainage

Manual drainage along the face and neck. The opening step that distinguishes Platinum from Signature and Deluxe.

Step 02

Cleanse

A thorough cleanse to prepare the skin for the deeper protocol that follows.

Step 03

Chemical peel

A measured peel calibrated by the practitioner to what the skin presents that day.

Step 04

Painless extraction

Extraction with the Hydrafacial wand, clearing congestion along the jawline and forehead.

Step 05

Antioxidant infusion

Antioxidant and peptide infusion to restore hydration and protect the skin barrier.

Step 06

LED phase

A closing red and blue LED phase. The face reads brighter immediately.

Common questions

Frequently asked

How does Platinum differ from the Signature and Deluxe?

Signature is thirty minutes and runs the core cleanse, exfoliate and hydrate sequence. Deluxe is forty-five and adds a booster step. Platinum runs sixty minutes and opens with manual lymphatic drainage along the face and neck before the full Hydrafacial sequence and a closing LED phase.

Will I see results immediately?

Yes. Hydrafacial is read into the skin in real time; the face is brighter, more even-toned and visibly hydrated by the closing minutes of the session. Deeper changes register across the days that follow as congestion clears.

Is there downtime?

No. Skin can read mildly flushed for an hour or two; tinted SPF and hydration are recommended for the rest of the day. Heavy actives and direct sun should be held back for forty-eight hours afterwards.

Can I add other skin work to the same day?

A Digital Skin Scan consultation pairs naturally before the session. Other extraction or peel work is held back for the day; the Wellness Advisor schedules adjacent skin sessions across separate days.

Where it is delivered

Jayasom Wellness Resort sits at AMAALA, on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast at Triple Bay. Hydrafacial Platinum is delivered on-property in the Spa facilities; one hour by car from Al Wajh airport.
