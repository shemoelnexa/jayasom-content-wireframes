Arrival to Departure

The shape of a day

One day flows into the next. Move through it step by step — discovery, introduction, treatment, rest — each leading to the one that follows.

Day One · Arrival

01 · Arrival

Pause. We’ve got you.

You'll arrive from a drive, or a flight, or perhaps both. Pause. We've got you. Breathe. Take a moment to ground. Time, and gravity, do their work. A restless pendulum moves ceaselessly across sand. Share tea. A silent meditation.

Here, silence becomes clarity. A deep breath, a pure sensation — the one thing that moves without noise.

Day One · Arrival

02 · Discovery Journey

An orientation, gently

A guided walk, or buggy ride, and orientation. The Farm Belt, the Health & Wellness Center, pools, dining, gardens, and viewing platforms — and on, to your villa or room.

The discovery walk

Day One · Arrival

03 · Wellness Introduction

Meeting your Wellness Advisor

Your first in-person meeting with your Wellness Advisor starts from the wellness reception. You may have already talked online, and if your wellness questionnaire is not already complete, this will be the time and place to do that. Your goals, your aspirations, and our opportunity to orchestrate the programming that will help fulfil them. Each retreat includes a range of assessments and treatments — but additionally, there are dozens of daily complimentary activities (the Daily Cadence) and supplemental treatments that may attract your interest.

Wellness reception

Day One · Arrival

04 · First Treatments

As the first evening settles

Depending on the time remaining in the first day, a treatment or an assessment may be scheduled. Take a stroll to appreciate the sunset and the colour of the evening reflected on the hills. And dine, early enough to allow your digestion to do its work before retiring.

Sunset over the hills

Finding the Rhythm

05 · First Night & New Morning

Options, but no rules

Awake, refreshed. Birdsong, or just silence. There are options but no rules. An early morning class — yoga, Tai Chi, stretching — or a more solitary moment — a stroll, a run, a meditation on the plateau. Breakfast is available; the Daily Cadence classes are on hand, and the rhythm of your own programme commences. The intensity is your choice.

Dawn on the plateau

Finding the Rhythm

06 · Assessments & Consultations

Patterns begin to emerge

A conversation. A few questions. Perhaps a movement assessment, a scan, blood test, or time with a practitioner who notices what you may have stopped noticing yourself. Patterns begin to emerge: how you sleep, where you hold tension, what gives you energy and what quietly takes it away. Each encounter adds another piece, building a fuller picture.

A quiet consultation

Finding the Rhythm

07 · Programme & Treatments

Your programme begins to live

Your programme begins to live. A treatment here, a private session there, with time in between to pause, move, eat or simply be. Some experiences are deeply restorative; others invite effort, curiosity or change. Nothing feels hurried. Your Wellness Advisor remains present in the background, making small adjustments as the retreat gathers its own rhythm.

A garden-facing treatment room

Finding the Rhythm

08 · Daily Cadence Options

A changing rhythm, around you

Around your personal programme, the Daily Cadence offers a changing rhythm of complimentary group activities. Join a movement class, explore breathwork or meditation, take part in a creative or cultural experience, or spend time outdoors. Choose what interests you and leave space where you need it. The cadence is there to support your stay, never to dictate it.

A movement class

Finding the Rhythm

09 · Rest & Relaxation

Not every moment needs a purpose

Not every moment needs a purpose. Rest beside the pool, return to the quiet of your room or villa, wander through the gardens, or pause at the beachfront as the light changes across the landscape. Sleep when you are tired. Read, float, reflect, or simply do nothing. These intervals are not separate from your retreat; they allow its benefits to settle, integrate and take hold.

Midday beside the pool

Onward

10 · And so on…

The days find their rhythm

The days find their rhythm. Morning light, movement, breakfast. A treatment, a conversation, a pause. Your phone becomes a distant memory; the urgency of elsewhere begins to soften. New habits feel less like resolutions and more like possibilities. You notice what restores you, what steadies you, and what you may no longer need.

Before departure, one final conversation with your Wellness Advisor. A moment to look back at what has shifted and consider what comes next. What can travel home with you: a practice, a different pace, a way of eating, moving or resting. And what can remain here, quietly left behind.

Slow & Mindful Dining

How the kitchens think

The kitchens hold an intentional approach: no gluten, no refined sugar, no coffee, and a small amount of salt in use. Flavour comes from the herbs and the technique. The cuisine celebrates the vibrant local flavours of the region, expertly blended with global influences, minimising gluten, refined sugars, excessive salt, coffee, and inflammatory agents. The Farm Belt feeds the menus where possible. The research on what foods support what conditions runs continuously, and the menu adjusts as the assessments and the seasons move.

The MOSO Hub

At the MOSO Hub

How the property holds itself

MOSO is the Chinese name for the giant bamboo, called Mao Zhu. It is among the fastest-growing plants on earth, and gives back more than it takes. The MOSO Hub is where the property tracks what it draws and what it returns: water cycles in the gardens, energy through the day, food miles to the kitchens, and the small measurements that hold the rhythm of the place. Sourcing decisions across the property are checked against what is recorded here.

Adult, Family, and Beyond

Pathways through the property

01

Jayana, the adult side

The Jayana day moves through the morning studio and the afternoon water: Yoga, Pranayama, restorative stretching, Mat Pilates, Pilates Reformer, Gyrokinesis, HIIT, Boot Camp, and aqua sessions in the cool of the mineral pool. Your Wellness Advisor reads the body each morning and shapes the order from there.

02

Jayela, the family side

The Jayela day moves through hands-on rooms and outdoor places designed for every age at once. Time at the MOSO Hub. Classes at the Nakeha Cooking Studio. Music and sound at the Flax Studio. Slow walking through the Colour Garden. Evenings in the Yasmeen Night Garden. Time on the Wellness Plateau and at the Stonecrop Maze. The four Children's Studios (Seed, Sprout, STEM, and Blossom) run age-appropriate sessions through the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How the day works

Does the schedule run on fixed times?

Yes, and no. Your personalised programme is established together with your Wellness Advisor according to your discussion and goals. The Daily Cadence is a set schedule of activities; you can choose to join as you wish (some classes require prior registration). Your wellness appointments, once established, are for specific times. Arriving late may result in a shorter session, as time extensions cannot be guaranteed.

Can families and adult guests share a day?

Your Wellness Advisor will help navigate your programme to support time together and independent time to the extent desired. Some group family activities are available that enable everyone to be together, whilst some other activities are more age aligned.
