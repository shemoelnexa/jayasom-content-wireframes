Executive wellness for leaders on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast. It feels like optimised performance, focus, and resilience.

Wellness Mentoring Consultation, Leadership

Wellness Mentoring Consultation, Leadership

Duration: 60 minutes

A goal-oriented session integrating evidence-based approaches including Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Neuro-Linguistic Programming, and HeartMath techniques. The leadership variant focuses on decision-rhythm under load and the discipline of clarity that high-stakes work depends on.

Shirodhara

Shirodhara

Duration: 90 minutes

The classic Ayurvedic Mind Calm. Warm medicated oil pours in a continuous stream over the third-eye point on the forehead, dissolving mental chatter and easing nervous-system load. Frequently chosen by leaders carrying decision fatigue or sleep disruption from intensive cycles.

Marma Point Therapy

Marma Point Therapy

Duration: 50 minutes

The Vitality Booster. A focused energy treatment targeting one hundred and seven Marma points, the junctions where matter and consciousness meet in Ayurvedic teaching. Applied with gentle pressure and essential oils, it works the points connected to mental clarity and stamina.

Heart-Focused Breathing & Biofeedback

Heart-Focused Breathing & Biofeedback

Duration: 50 minutes plus 30-minute follow-up

A heart-focused breathwork protocol paired with biofeedback. The session reads heart-rate variability and uses the data to inform nervous-system pacing across the stay. Leaders use the protocol to recalibrate stress response between high-stakes cycles.

Pinda Sweda

Pinda Sweda (Ayurvedic Herbal Compress)

Duration: 90 minutes

A therapeutic treatment for joint and muscle relief. Warm linen bags filled with medicinal herbs and rice are dipped in heated oil and worked across the body. Particularly effective for desk-bound stiffness and the muscular signature of long flights.

Pranayama

Pranayama

Duration: 50 minutes

The Ayurvedic practice of controlled breathing. Guided breathwork slows the heart rate and settles the nervous system into the kind of spacious mental quiet that decision-making depends on.

Watsu, Aquatic Therapy

Watsu, Aquatic Therapy

Duration: 50 minutes

Passive water therapy in a warm pool. The therapist gently stretches and mobilises the body to release tension and ease discomfort. Particularly relevant for leaders carrying chronic muscular tension from the desk or accumulated travel load.

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep Tissue Massage

Duration: 50 or 90 minutes

Targeted bodywork that breaks down scar tissue and fibrous connective tissue to improve blood flow and reduce pain. A course of three sessions across the stay provides the strongest result.

The approach

Methodology, distinction, assessment

The work draws on the Wellness Wisdom® pillar of the Jayasom system. Beneath that sits the Ground Wellbeing practice, the named treatment system through which the work happens. Threaded through both is Traditional Arabic Medicine (TAIM, also known as Tibb), the regional heritage that anchors Jayasom's clinical approach in the Kingdom. This combination, TAIM heritage clinical practice integrated with contemporary evidence-based methods (CBT, NLP, HeartMath, HRV biofeedback), is the operating intersection that distinguishes Jayasom in the Kingdom and the wider region.

The methodology recognises that leaders arrive carrying different loads at different cycles; the same body needs different support at each. Clinical assessment opens every stay, run as a one-to-one consultation with the resident-clinician on day one and revisited at the closing conversation. Around that assessment, the daily rhythm of bodywork, movement, and rest is calibrated to where you are now and the demands you are returning to. A meaningful digital detox runs across the days, with protected reflection windows built into the timetable.

The setting

The Setting at AMAALA

Jayasom AMAALA sits within the Triple Bay master plan on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast. The property holds sixty villas plus twenty-four private residences. Phase one is intentionally small for the destination it sits within; the daily rhythm is sized to the cohort, not to the building. Triple Bay is part of the AMAALA destination, a regional flagship for executive wellness in the Kingdom.

Yasmeen Night Garden runs in scent-only mode through the evening hours, designed for slow walking. Stonecrop Maze sits on the Wellness Plateau and is open through the late afternoon. Phoenix Garden holds private daytime rest. Sound Healing Therapy runs at the Sonorium. Within the Jayana zone, the As a Leader programme operates with adult-side privacy and protected reflection hours.

Cultural fluency runs through the property: privacy and respectful discretion operate as daily standards, with cultural sensitivity built into how the timetable is shaped. Photography is at the guest's discretion. Private entrances and protected schedules are available where requested. Communications stay private through the booking flow; family-shared accounts can be replaced with a private channel for the booking. The property's quiet hours are honoured across the programme, supporting the digital detox that grounds the work.

Three named tracks

Programme by Leadership Phase

The programme adjusts to the cycle you arrive in. Three named tracks hold the structure; your Wellness Advisor selects the right combination at the pre-arrival conversation.

Executive Recovery Track

For leaders arriving from intensive cycles, board seasons, or transition moments. The Wellness Mentoring Consultation Leadership sits at the centre. Shirodhara, Pinda Sweda, and the Sleep Ritual support recovery; Heart-Focused Breathing and Biofeedback recalibrates the nervous system; Pranayama and Meditation hold the daily breath work. Movement runs measured: Tai Chi, Somatic Stretch, Personal Training calibrated to the energy you arrive with.

Clarity and Decision Track

For leaders preparing for major decisions or high-cognitive-load periods ahead, with transitions often layered through both. Marma Point Therapy and Shirodhara anchor the cognitive-clarity layer; Heart-Focused Breathing and Biofeedback delivers measurable nervous-system data; Watsu and Far Infrared Therapy support the deeper recovery. Movement adds intensity: Kick Boxing, Super Stretch, Gyrotonic.

Performance Optimisation Track

For leaders extending peak capacity over longer cycles. Daily Treatments rotate through the named bodywork roster (Deep Tissue, Marma Point, Foot Reflexology, Thai Traditional Massage). Personal Training and Neurac Method work the physical layer. The Nutrition & Lifestyle Consultation anchors fuelling and recovery through the stay.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best executive wellness retreat in Saudi Arabia?

Jayasom AMAALA's As a Leader retreat sits on the Red Sea coast within the Triple Bay master plan, integrating Traditional Arabic Medicine with contemporary wellness science. It runs four to fourteen nights and is shaped at arrival by your Wellness Advisor. Three programme tracks anchor the work: Executive Recovery; Clarity and Decision; and Performance Optimisation.

How long should I stay for a leadership wellness retreat?

Stays run from four to fourteen nights, adaptable to schedule and intent. Four nights to Explore, seven nights to Absorb, ten nights to Immerse, fourteen nights to Commit. Your Wellness Advisor will recommend a duration during the pre-arrival conversation, calibrated to the cycle you arrive in and the demands you are returning to.

Can I bring my C-suite team?

Yes. As a Leader is structured for both individual and team bookings. The Residences pathway suits C-suite cohorts; the Wellness Advisor coordinates the programme across the group while preserving the privacy each leader needs.

What's the digital detox like, and is it strict?

Devices stay accessible through specific windows. The rest of the day is built around protected reflection time. Your Wellness Advisor sets the cadence with you at the pre-arrival call so it fits the leadership cycle you are in.

What kinds of leaders does this retreat work for?

The retreat is calibrated for individuals and C-suite teams across sectors: family business chairs, executives in transition, founder-leaders, board members, mentors. Clarity under pressure unifies them, and the discipline to sustain it across the cycles each role demands. The programme is designed first for GCC leadership audiences with cultural fluency at the centre; international leaders find the same care, calibrated to their context.
