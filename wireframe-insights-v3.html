How we write

Each piece carries an author and a date. We don't ghost-write under the brand name; the people writing get the byline. We name the practitioners we cite, link the studies we draw on, and update older pieces when the practice moves. The point is to be useful, not to be everywhere.

Editorial direction from Carol El Hadi, Director of Marketing, PR and Communications. Each article carries its own author byline.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Blogs and Journals at Jayasom?

Blogs are research-led and answer search-driven questions about wellness, longevity, and practice. Journals are immersive and human; they document what life at the property actually looks like.

Who writes the Insights articles?

The wellness team, the practitioners in residence, and the editorial team at Jayasom. Each piece carries the author's byline and date.

How often is the content updated?

Featured updates weekly. New writing lands across the streams as it's ready; we don't run a fixed publishing calendar. The newsletter goes out monthly.

Who writes the Thought Leadership pieces?

Pieces are authored by named members of the Jayasom team and named visiting practitioners. Each Thought Leadership entry carries an author bio and a reviewed-by line; named credentials are visible in the byline.

How are Journal pieces different from Blog pieces?

Journal pieces are immersive entries from life at the wellness destination, often a single morning or a single retreat moment. Blog pieces sit closer to research and longevity science. The streams are filtered separately.
