We share what we know, what we are learning, and what the people here believe. These pages are our thinking and an invitation into a community that puts well-being at the heart of everything they do.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between blogs and journals at Jayasom?

The blog is where we explore questions about sleep, longevity, nutrition, movement, and what well-being looks like in practice. The journals are something different. They are written from inside a day at Jayasom AMAALA - a morning on the water, a session with the Wellness Advisor, the feeling of a place that asks you to slow down.

Who writes the Insights articles?

Our wellness team, resident specialists, visiting practitioners, and the people who built and run Jayasom. The writing comes from inside the experience — by the people you'll meet when you're here.

How often is the content updated?

New pieces are published as they are ready rather than on a fixed schedule. If you would like to know when something new arrives, the newsletter is the simplest way to stay close.

Who writes the Perspectives pieces?

The people inside Jayasom who have something worth saying — the founders, the specialists, the visiting practitioners and doctors who work with guests every day. They write from their own experience and belief.

Can I share a perspective or contribute to Insights?

Jayasom is a global platform, and we believe that wisdom about well-being exists in every region and culture. If you have something to contribute - a perspective rooted in your practice, your community, or your part of the world, we would love to hear from you. Reach out through our contact page.
