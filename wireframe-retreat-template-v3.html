Programme inclusions

What's Included

Stay length shapes the depth of the work. From the methodology, four tier names hold the structure: four nights to Explore, seven nights to Absorb, ten nights to Immerse, fourteen nights to Commit. Pacing matches the tier; nothing across the stay is added because of the calendar.

Every [retreat name] stay includes

  • Accommodation in your chosen room category, with daily housekeeping
  • Three wellness cuisine meals per night, beginning with dinner on the day of arrival
  • Wellness minibar in your room
  • Individual health and wellness consultation with your Wellness Advisor
  • Physical analysis (optional)
  • Skin consultation (optional)
  • Daily fitness and leisure activities (up to six classes daily)
  • Unlimited use of all wellness destination facilities (steam, sauna, mineral and vitality pools, salt inhalation rooms, infrared rooms, mixed gym)
  • One daily treatment per night of stay, chosen from a seven-treatment menu (Jayasom Signature Massage, Balinese, Deep Tissue, Classical Swedish, Chakra Balancing, Foot Reflexology, Abhyanga)
  • Physical and Postural Assessment plus VISIA Facial Imaging, complimentary on request
  • The Jayasom Collection (welcome amenity)

Personalisation note

Programme inclusions are indicative; alternatives are available; your Wellness Advisor finalises the programme shortly after arrival, around your individual needs and stated objectives.

Services and facilities are subject to government health regulations.
