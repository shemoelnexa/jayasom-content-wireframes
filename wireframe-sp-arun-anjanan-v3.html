About

Arun's understanding of health has been shaped by years spent caring for others, and a growing appreciation for the connection between physical wellbeing, emotional balance and the rhythms of everyday life. What began with a foundation in nursing gradually evolved into a broader interest in preventive health and holistic living — leading him to approaches that recognise the individual as a whole, rather than focusing solely on symptoms.

Throughout his journey, Arun has come to appreciate that wellbeing is rarely defined by dramatic changes. He finds meaning instead in the small, consistent practices that support vitality over time. His calm and reassuring presence reflects an understanding that listening, patience and compassion are often just as important as knowledge and expertise.

At Jayasom, Arun brings together clinical understanding and a thoughtful outlook, creating space for each individual to slow down, reconnect and rediscover habits that feel both nourishing and sustainable. He values simple rituals, and believes that caring for oneself begins with kindness, intention and an awareness of the many ways health touches everyday life.

People, not programmes

Speak to a Wellness Advisor

Healing at Jayasom happens through people. The practitioners here arrived at wellness through their own journeys — paths that moved through other lives and other disciplines before finding their way here, carrying the kind of knowledge that only comes from having walked some of the way yourself. Visiting Practitioners join for specific residencies, adding depth that shifts what those weeks can hold. The Wellness Advisor speaks with you before you arrive, reads the body on the day you walk in, and draws on everyone here to shape something around what they find — refining it as the days settle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Wellness Advisor at Jayasom?

Your Wellness Advisor is a qualified specialist who builds and holds your retreat plan across the stay.

Who are the Visiting Practitioners?

Experts who join the team for specific periods. They conduct treatments and sessions that are not on the year-round calendar.

Can I request a specific practitioner?

Speak to the Wellness Advisor in your pre-arrival consultation. Where a practitioner is in residence and the schedule allows, the request is honoured. Where it isn't, the Wellness Advisor will suggest the closest available.
