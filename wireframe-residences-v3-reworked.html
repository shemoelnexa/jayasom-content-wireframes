Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly are Jayasom Residences located?

Within Jayasom's AMAALA Triple Bay master plan, at the wellness core of Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast — about one hour from Al Wajh Domestic Airport (EJH) and under two hours from Red Sea International (RSI) and NEOM Bay.

How many residences are available, and what configurations?

Twenty-four in total: ten two-bedroom (350.2 m²), twelve three-bedroom (423.9 m²), and two four-bedroom (525.3 m²). Variations by floor, view and garden access; each has a private pool.

Who designed the residences?

Architecture by Heah & Co — receding volumes set low against the coastline; interiors by Studio Carter, with calibrated natural materials and a composed, quietly held aesthetic.

Can owners use Jayasom wellness facilities?

Owners receive Wellness Membership during stays — with Wellness Advisor access, treatment rooms, the dining studio, the gardens and the wider AMAALA amenities. Access frequency and structure are detailed in the ownership package.

Is there a rental programme when I am not in residence?

Yes — a managed rental programme, with the operations team handling bookings, housekeeping, maintenance and the guest experience. Net yields and revenue split are detailed under NDA on enquiry.
