About

Lujain Abdullah AlHussan is a Wellness Mentor and Psychological Counsellor with extensive experience in family counselling, organisational mental health, and digital therapy. She brings a thoughtful, evidence-based approach to psychological care, supporting individuals, families, and communities through tailored therapeutic programmes that promote resilience, emotional balance, and long-term wellbeing.

Lujain holds a Bachelor's degree in Psychology and a Higher Diploma in Family Counselling, and has worked across diverse clinical and community settings in Saudi Arabia, including healthcare institutions, national initiatives, and teletherapy platforms. Her work covers individual therapy, family systems support, and mental health awareness programmes, often in collaboration with multidisciplinary teams. She delivers culturally sensitive care using evidence-based modalities such as Cognitive Behavioural Therapy and Dialectical Behaviour Therapy.

At Jayasom, Lujain brings a distinct psychological lens to the guest experience. She works past the immediate request, holding each guest's broader emotional life in view. Saudi cultural warmth and psychological insight sit together in her mentoring; the result is personal, intuitive, and anticipatory care, attentive to both the spoken and the unspoken.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Empathetic Presence?

Lujain's personal philosophy. She defines it as understanding both the spoken and the unspoken needs of guests, blending the warmth of Saudi culture with psychological insight.

What therapeutic modalities does Lujain use?

Evidence-based modalities including Cognitive Behavioural Therapy and Dialectical Behaviour Therapy, adapted to each guest's emotional needs and cultural context.

Which retreats does Lujain anchor?

She is the named anchor of As a Pair, and contributes psychological counselling across As a Woman and As a Leader.

Can I book a session with Lujain directly?

Sessions with Lujain are arranged through your Wellness Advisor, who shapes a programme around your goals during your stay.
