Design Exploration

The shape of a day — two simple layouts

The same five-step journey, shown two ways. Compare and pick a direction. (The main page currently uses Option B, the slider.)

Option A

Five cards

Everything visible at once — no clicking. Arrival leads as a wider card; the other four sit in a simple grid.

01 · 5

Arrival

You arrive from a drive or a flight, or perhaps both. Pause, breathe, and take a moment to ground. Share tea, a silent meditation — time and gravity do their work.

Here, silence becomes clarity. A deep breath, a pure sensation — the one thing that moves without noise.

02 · 5

Discovery & introduction

A guided walk or buggy ride orients you to the property — the Farm Belt, the Health & Wellness Center, pools, dining and gardens. Then your first meeting with your Wellness Advisor shapes the programme around your goals.

03 · 5

First treatments & new mornings

A first treatment or assessment as the evening settles, a sunset stroll, an unhurried dinner. Wake to options but no rules — an early class or a quiet moment, then breakfast and the Daily Cadence. The intensity is your choice.

04 · 5

Your programme comes to life

Conversations, scans and movement assessments build a fuller picture — how you sleep, where you hold tension, what restores you. Your programme begins to live: treatments and private sessions, with time in between to pause, move, or simply be.

05 · 5

Rhythm, rest, and onward

The Daily Cadence offers complimentary classes around your programme, and quiet hours hold space to rest. The days find their rhythm, and before departure a final conversation considers what travels home with you — and what can remain, quietly left behind.
