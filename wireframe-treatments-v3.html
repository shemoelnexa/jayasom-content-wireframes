Treatments

Sixty treatments, each chosen for what it does, not how it sounds.

The sixty published here are the ones that earn their place. Three things qualify them. The Wellness Advisors prescribe them most often. The practitioners are accredited to deliver them. And the rituals hold up across a week of daily use.

Speak with a Wellness Advisor

Sixty treatments are published on the website, drawn from a wider operational catalogue of more than two hundred. The published cohort is the working set for retreat guests; the wider catalogue surfaces through the Wellness Advisor where it fits a programme.

Treatments fall across four categories: massage and bodywork, Ayurvedic protocols, Traditional Arabic and Islamic Medicine, and contemporary clinical care. Each draws on a Therapeutic Pillar and is anchored by a named practitioner.

Each treatment page carries a duration, a practitioner anchor, the Therapeutic Pillar it belongs to, and a contraindication note where relevant. Booking sits with your Wellness Advisor rather than direct-self-service.

Treatment Directory

A wider catalogue beneath the published cohort

Jayasom AMAALA's Treatment Directory carries one hundred and fifty treatments across the Therapeutic Pillars of body, mind and spirit. Some anchor a single retreat; many thread across multiple retreat shapes. The Wellness Advisor places each against the brief at arrival, reading the body's starting point and the goals the stay is asking for.

Treatments cluster across categories: Wellness Assessments and clinical baselines; Sound and energetic therapies; Bodywork and manual practice; Hydrotherapy and water-borne work; Ayurvedic protocols; Traditional Arabic and Islamic Medicine; Beauty and skin care; and Recovery and active rehabilitation.

Anchor treatments differ by retreat. The Couple's Connect Spa Ritual sits within As a Pair; QALBAIN, separately, sits as a couples treatment alongside it. Watsu, Marma Point, and Pinda Sweda thread across multiple retreat shapes. The daily 60-minute treatment from the seven-treatment menu (Jayasom Signature Massage, Balinese, Deep Tissue, Classical Swedish, Chakra Balancing, Foot Reflexology, Abhyanga) runs across every retreat.

Every retreat opens with a one-to-one Health Consultation, plus a Specialist Consultation calibrated to the retreat shape. Physical and Postural Assessment plus VISIA Facial Imaging are complimentary on request. The Wellness Mentoring sessions thread across longer stays for guests carrying a longer-arc question.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How many treatments are available at Jayasom AMAALA?

Sixty treatments are published. The full operational catalogue runs to more than one hundred and fifty; the Wellness Advisor draws on the wider set when a guest's plan needs it.

What are the Therapeutic Pillars?

Three groupings: Body work, Health work, and Aesthetic. Most retreat weeks draw from all three; the Wellness Advisor decides the weighting.

Can I book treatments without a retreat?

Day-guest treatments are available subject to capacity. The booking team confirms availability per date; the Wellness Advisor consultation is included.

Do treatments include consultations?

A Wellness Advisor consultation is included with any retreat booking and with most stand-alone treatment bookings of more than ninety minutes.
