The Intention

When Sleep Itself Needs Restoring

As You Rest is created for those wishing to improve sleep quality, restore evening routines and wake feeling more refreshed – combining medical insight with restorative practices and functional lifestyle guidance. The length of As You Rest aligns with your chosen retreat.

As You Rest is individually tailored and may include sleep assessments, specialist consultations and, where appropriate, diagnostic studies to better understand contributing factors. Evidence-informed approaches – including cognitive behavioural coaching for insomnia – are complemented by movement practices, breathwork and relaxation techniques that encourage a more settled nervous system.

Nutritional guidance, restorative therapies, hydrotherapy, sound-based practices and periods of digital disconnection help create supportive conditions for rest. The emphasis throughout is on understanding your individual needs and developing realistic routines that support healthier sleep over time.

How As You Rest takes shape

How As You Rest Takes Shape

As You Rest opens with a sleep-focused consultation – a review of your history, patterns, contributing factors and lifestyle context. Where appropriate, diagnostic studies support a more detailed picture before your Wellness Advisor builds your plan.

Your days draw on a considered mix of evidence-informed and restorative approaches: cognitive behavioural coaching for insomnia, movement and breathwork, sound-based practices and therapeutic rest. Nutritional guidance and periods of digital disconnection are woven into your schedule to support the conditions for sleep, not just manage the symptoms of poor rest.

You leave with a clear picture of what your sleep needs and a practical set of habits to carry home.

Questions

FAQs

What is As You Rest?

As You Rest is a sleep and recovery add-on available alongside any Jayasom AMAALA signature retreat. It layers targeted sleep support – clinical assessment, restorative therapies and evidence-informed coaching – onto your existing retreat to address sleep quality, energy and nervous system recovery.

Who is As You Rest suitable for?

Any guest experiencing disrupted sleep, difficulty switching off, or low energy on waking – and those who want to use their time at Jayasom to actively improve their sleep health. An arrival consultation confirms the most appropriate approach for you.

How long does As You Rest run for?

As You Rest aligns with your chosen signature retreat – 4, 7, 10 or 14 nights. It is not available as a standalone stay.

Can I add it to any retreat?

Yes. As You Rest can be added to any of the six Jayasom AMAALA signature retreats. Your Wellness Advisor will sequence both in a way that supports your overall well-being goals.

Does it include a medical assessment?

Yes. As You Rest may include a clinical sleep assessment and, where appropriate, diagnostic studies to identify contributing factors. Findings inform your personalised plan and are reviewed throughout your stay.
