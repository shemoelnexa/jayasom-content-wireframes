The Daily Cadence

A daily programme of activities, with something for everyone.

Beyond your retreat

The Daily Cadence is a schedule of classes and elective wellness activities offered throughout the day — open to you alongside your retreat programme.

Try something new; explore and discover. Sessions run from sunrise movement to sound meditation after dark, so choose the ones that interest you. Every class is tailored to accommodate different abilities, and those that are advanced are clearly indicated.

At a glance

  • Most classes complimentary — included in your retreat
  • No booking needed, unless a fee or capacity limit applies
  • All abilities welcome; advanced classes are flagged
  • Some sessions have limited places — reserve ahead

Dynamic Class Schedule

Live in the Jayasom app · reserve through your Wellness Concierge.

The classes rotate and the instructors sometimes change, but what stays steady is the structure of the day: a morning movement window, a midday stretch that anchors the schedule, an afternoon strength block, and an evening practice that brings everything to a close. Times are confirmed at the front desk on arrival and refreshed each morning.

Where

Where activities happen

Adults practise across the Health and Wellness Centre, the gardens, the wellness plateau and the pool deck; some sessions move outside depending on the weather and the instructor. The Children's programme runs through the four Growing Studios — Seed, Sprout, Stem and Blossom Lounge — plus Buttercup Play Space, Pool Play and Sand Play on the Family side. Watersports and beach sessions launch from the Beach Hut.

The venue for each activity is shown on the Daily Cadence and in the Jayasom app.

Pricing & Fees

Most activities are complimentary — included within your retreat. In some cases, where a charge applies, this is noted in the Daily Cadence and advised when you reserve.

Private Options

Most group classes can also be arranged as one-on-one private sessions, or for couples and family groups. Contact your Wellness Advisor to make arrangements.

In residence

Visiting Practitioners run additional classes

When a Visiting Practitioner is in residence, the schedule expands. Past residencies have included sound healing intensives, breath workshops and partner-yoga clinics. Their sessions are added to the calendar automatically for the days they are here.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What group activities are available at Jayasom AMAALA?

The Daily Cadence shows the programme of activities for the day and the week. There are dozens of different activities — fitness, meditative, creative, educational and sustainability sessions — something for everyone.

Do I need to book the classes?

Booking is not required unless there is a payable fee or a limit on the number of participants. The Daily Cadence will indicate where a booking is required.

Can children join adult classes?

Some specified classes are intended for adults and children together. Outside that window, adults practise in the adult programme and children practise in age-appropriate sessions in the Growing Studios. Some Visiting Practitioners run cross-age workshops when they are in residence.

Are sessions adapted for beginners?

Yes. The instructor reads the room at the start of each session and adjusts accordingly. If you are new to a practice, mention it before the class begins. Sessions that require prior experience will be indicated as such.
