Destination

Triple Bay, AMAALA

Triple Bay lies on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast, in a region of mountains, warm sand beaches, coral reefs, and the clear blue of the Red Sea. A Marina Village holds the northern end of AMAALA; a walking route leads south to the quieter wellness core, where Jayasom sits at the edge of the sea facing a serene cove. The light, the salt air, the sea breeze, the temperature swing between day and evening; and the stillness. Each playing a part in how the body settles.

Rooms with Views

A choice of villas and rooms offers different settings, outlooks and levels of privacy to suit your needs.

Pool villa

Pool Villas

Most accommodation takes the form of private pool villas, ranging from one to five bedrooms. They are set in a variety of locations, from the beachfront and hilltop to gently elevated sites and tranquil gardens.

Terrace room

Terrace Rooms

Terrace Rooms enjoy spectacular sea views and offer a more accessible alternative without a private pool. They are particularly well suited to young families and guests seeking greater ease of mobility.

Sustainability

Sustainable Living

At Jayasom AMAALA, personal wellbeing and planetary health are inseparable, inspiring a way of living that protects and restores the natural world.

Designed with Responsibility

Renewable energy, water conservation, living gardens and LEED-certified design are woven into our fabric. Responsible choices shape both how Jayasom AMAALA operates and how guests experience each day.

Nature, First-Hand

Sustainability comes to life through meaningful encounters. Explore the Farm Belt, hydrate from our reusable flask, discover onsite water bottling, or witness the rare sight of turtles nesting along the nearby beach.

See the Difference

The Moso Hub offers a transparent window into our environmental commitments, from resource reporting to active conservation initiatives. We invite everyone to learn, participate and help nurture a healthier future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Jayasom AMAALA opens soon in 2026. Subscribe for more information, or follow our socials.

When does Jayasom AMAALA open?

Phase one opens in 2026. Enquiries are taken now; your Wellness Advisor shapes your stay from there.

How do I get to Jayasom AMAALA?

Al Wajh Domestic Airport (EJH) is 50 minutes by car. Red Sea International (RSI) is approximately 2 hours away, while Neom Bay Airport (NUM) is approximately 2.5 hours away. Transfer is arranged as part of the booking.

What's the booking process?

An enquiry opens a conversation with your Wellness Advisor. Your week is built around what your body asks for, not a fixed schedule.

How do I book?

Use the button at the top right of the webpage. You'll select your dates, a retreat and then your room. If you're not sure which retreat is the best place to start, book a complementary call with a Wellness Advisor to explore further.
