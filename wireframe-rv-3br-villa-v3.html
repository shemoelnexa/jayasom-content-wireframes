Design

A home that breathes to the rhythms of the Red Sea

Heah & Co drew the architecture. The brief was to balance modernism with organic contextual design, prioritising the natural beauty of the setting and instilling a deep connection with the landscape. Open, light-filled spaces emphasise simplicity and serenity; indigenous materials and exceptional local craftsmanship carry a distinct sense of place. Studio Carter shaped the interiors. Spacious yet cocooning, they express the lyrical essence of the surrounding naturescapes and the cultural wealth of this ancient region.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How many bedrooms in a three-bedroom beach villa?

Three bedrooms, each with en-suite bathroom and dressing area, plus a separate staff bedroom. Total area is 423.9 sqm across 26 numbered rooms.

What is included with the villa?

Private 82.32 sqm pool, sea-facing terrace, direct beach access, family-sized dining and lounge, kitchen with Miele appliances, staff quarters, garage. Smart Home control throughout.

What is the wellness programme for residence owners?

The Jayasom Longevity Assessment Programme: an appointed personal Wellness Advisor, blood and micronutrient analysis once a year, fitness, postural, kinesthetic, skin and biofeedback assessments twice a year.

Can the villa be rented when I am not in residence?

Yes. Owners can include their residence in the resort rental programme, managed by the Jayasom team while owners are not in residence.

Where is the villa located?

At Jayasom Wellness Residences, part of Triple Bay, AMAALA, on the Red Sea coast of Saudi Arabia. Al Wajh airport is one hour by car.
