Our Practitioners

The resident practitioners who hold the rhythm of your stay — advisors, therapists, nurses and physiotherapists who bring care, presence and quiet attention to every day.

Health & Wellness Advisor

Arun Anjanan

With a background in nursing and holistic care, Arun brings a calm and reassuring presence to every interaction. He values the importance of listening and understanding, helping each individual find a rhythm that feels right for them. Combining clinical knowledge with genuine compassion, he supports moments of restoration that feel personal, thoughtful and grounded in everyday life.

Health & Wellness Advisor

Jennifer Abou Chaaya

Jennifer sees nourishment as something that extends far beyond food. Through thoughtful conversations and practical guidance, she helps individuals build a more mindful relationship with eating and daily habits. Her work is shaped by curiosity, empathy and the understanding that lasting change often begins with the smallest of choices.

Health & Wellness Advisor

Nandu Sreekumar

Rooted in the traditions of Ayurveda, Nandu brings together ancient wisdom and a contemporary understanding of health. He takes time to understand the individual, encouraging balance through practices that honour the natural rhythms of life. His quiet manner and thoughtful perspective invite a sense of ease that unfolds gently over time.

Health & Wellness Advisor

Hanen Lammouchi

Hanen's work reflects her appreciation for the connection between inner vitality and outer confidence. With warmth and attentiveness, she creates experiences that encourage self-care and moments of pause. Her gentle presence helps others reconnect with themselves in ways that feel both restorative and deeply personal.

Psychologist

Mnahel Ali Alhumaidan

Mnahel offers a space where reflection and understanding can unfold naturally. Through meaningful conversations and compassionate support, she helps individuals navigate life's challenges with greater clarity and self-awareness. Her work is grounded in the belief that caring for the mind is an essential part of living with balance and purpose.

Child Development Facilitator

Jehad Malibari

Jehad creates spaces where families can slow down, connect and simply enjoy being together. Drawing on her background in psychology, she encourages curiosity, creativity and joyful discovery through experiences designed for children and their loved ones. Her warm and playful nature brings a sense of ease that allows meaningful moments to unfold naturally.

Holistic Therapist

Shamjid Ebrahim

Inspired by traditional healing philosophies, Shamjid weaves together touch, sound and energy practices in a way that feels intuitive and deeply grounding. His sessions invite moments of stillness and quiet reflection, creating space for rest and reconnection. Guided by a lifelong interest in ancient wisdom, he brings a gentle sense of presence to his work.

Holistic Therapist

Jasleen Nokwal

Movement, breath and sound are at the heart of Jasleen's practice. Whether through yoga or meditative experiences, she creates moments that encourage stillness and awareness. Her nurturing nature and inclusive spirit allow people of all ages to feel comfortable exploring practices that bring greater balance and calm into daily life.

Wellness Nurse

Abdullah Bawazir

Abdullah brings warmth and attentiveness to every interaction, creating an environment where people feel comfortable and cared for. With a special interest in digestive health and restorative therapies, he values the importance of making each experience feel relaxed, supportive and deeply personal.

Wellness Nurse

Abdulrahman Qurban

Thoughtful and quietly attentive, Abdulrahman approaches care with sincerity and kindness. He understands that reassurance often lies in the smallest gestures and takes pride in creating a sense of comfort that allows others to feel supported and at ease throughout their stay.

Wellness Nurse

Mazen Althagafi

Mazen brings patience and quiet confidence to his role, believing that care is expressed through presence and attention to detail. His calm nature creates an atmosphere that encourages rest and ease, allowing individuals to focus on themselves and the moments that matter most.

Wellness Nurse

Sondos Alharbi

Sondos is guided by compassion and a genuine desire to care for others. Her gentle nature and attentive manner create a reassuring presence that helps people feel seen, heard and supported. She values the human connections that lie at the heart of every healing experience.

Physiotherapist

Mohammed Almslmani

For Mohammed, movement is a lifelong companion. He enjoys helping people rediscover confidence in their bodies through mindful rehabilitation and functional movement. His work reflects a belief that strength and mobility are not simply goals, but part of living fully and embracing everyday life with greater ease.

Physiotherapist

Yusuf Meemesh

Yusuf takes a thoughtful and measured view of movement, recognising that every individual has their own pace. Through attentive care and a genuine understanding of physical recovery, he helps people find comfort in movement and reconnect with the activities they enjoy most.

Physiotherapist

Aqeela Hakami

Drawn to the relationship between movement and mindful living, Aqeela enjoys helping others reconnect with their bodies through gentle and purposeful practices. Her interest in Pilates and functional movement reflects a belief that caring for the body can be both restorative and joyful, creating a greater sense of balance in everyday life.
