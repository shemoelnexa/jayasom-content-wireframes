Is airport pickup included?

Yes. We arrange airport pickup with every reservation. The nearest international airport is Al Wajh, one hour by car.

Are the residences available for purchase as well as rental?

Yes. Residences are available for ownership and for rental within the Rental Programme. Read more on the Wellness Residences page or use the Investment and Ownership block.
