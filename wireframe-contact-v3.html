Common Questions

Frequently asked: booking and enquiries

How quickly will I receive a reply?

We answer enquiries within two working days. Reservations enquiries route directly to our reservations team. Press, supplier, and specialist enquiries route to the relevant team within Jayasom.

Where is Jayasom located?

Jayasom Wellness Resort sits at AMAALA, Triple Bay, on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast. The nearest international airport is Al Wajh, one hour by car.

Is airport pickup included?

Yes. We arrange airport pickup with every reservation. Tell us your flight details once your booking is confirmed.

How do I make a reservation?

Use the BOOK NOW button in the global header, or write to amaala.reservations@jayasom.com. Our reservations team will guide you through retreat selection and arrival arrangements.

Can I visit Jayasom for a day or short stay?

Our retreat tiers begin at four nights. A three-night Taste of Jayasom intro product is available separately.

Do you accept group enquiries or wellness collaborations?

Yes. Select "Other" or "Development Interest" from the dropdown, or write directly to amaala.marketing@jayasom.com with a short note about your enquiry.
