Group Activities

Daily classes that turn up whether you've booked them or not.

Twenty-two classes run across the week, from sunrise yoga to sound healing after dark. The sessions are open to all guests in residence; no booking, no extra fee. The schedule shifts where it needs to: an activity might not be available because somebody goes on leave or gets pregnant. We tell you what's running on the day.

A note on time

We don't run on the clock

We don't need a timeline. The classes change. The instructors change. The same Yin Yoga session won't be the same in June as in March. What stays steady: a morning movement window, a stretch class at the middle of the day, an afternoon strength session, and an evening meditation. The names are listed below; the times are confirmed at the front desk on arrival.

For adults

The Adults Schedule

The adult schedule has six daily windows: a sunrise movement class, a mid-morning aqua session, a midday stretch class that runs every day, an afternoon strength or HIIT block, an early-evening breath or meditation practice, and a closing session before sleep. Twenty-two classes rotate through these windows across the week.

The Adults Daily Activity Calendar runs twenty-two named classes across the week: yoga, tai chi, breath, meditation, dance, sound, somatic work and outdoor practice. Each class is open to retreat guests and residents.

For children

The Children's Schedule

Children have their own programme, run by the Family team across the Growing Studios. Five daily slots, forty minutes each, across five age bands. Activities rotate; thirty-five sessions across the week. The universal Family slot at the late afternoon brings parents and children together.

Ages 0–3

Seed Studio

Sensory play, calm rhythms, parent-paired sessions.

Ages 4–8

Sprout Studio

Movement, music, and small-group exploration.

Ages 9–12

Stem Lounge

Skill-building, projects, and group games.

Ages 13+

Bloom or Blossom Lounge

Studio space, VR treadmills, longer-format projects.

Universal · 16:00–17:00

Family Slot

Parents and children together; everyone welcome.

The Children's Calendar runs in parallel: thirty-five activities across five daily slots and seven days, with a universal sixteen-hundred to seventeen-hundred Family slot every day. Activity duration is forty minutes, designed to fit alongside adult programmes. Children's activities are tagged across thirty skill-development categories spanning physical, social, cognitive and creative growth.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What group activities are available at Jayasom AMAALA?

Twenty-two adult classes and thirty-five children's activities across the week, grouped into yoga, breath, dance, sound, aqua, meditation, strength, stretch, Pilates, and Tai Chi. The exact schedule shifts; we confirm it daily.

Do I need to book the classes?

No. Group activities are open to all guests in residence; no booking, no extra fee. Capacity is generous; if a session is full at the door, the next one is on the same week's calendar.

Can children join adult classes?

The Family slot in the late afternoon is designed for parents and children together. Outside that, adults practise in adult sessions and children practise in age-banded sessions. Some Visiting Practitioners run cross-age workshops; these are flagged on the calendar.

Are sessions adapted for beginners?

Most sessions take all levels. The instructor sets the day's intensity from who turns up. Tell them at the start if you're new to a practice; they'll cue accordingly.

Group activities are held across the Jayasom Wellness Resort at AMAALA on the Red Sea, Triple Bay, Saudi Arabia. One hour by car from Al Wajh airport.
