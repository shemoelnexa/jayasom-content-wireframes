Mohammad Almslmany

Book a wellness consultation Back to all specialists

Mohammad Almslmany is a physiotherapist with a strong background in rehabilitation, fitness training, and patient-centred care. With experience across healthcare facilities and large-scale wellness projects in Saudi Arabia, Mohammad brings a practical, results-driven approach to physical therapy, focused on restoring movement, improving function, and supporting long-term wellbeing.

Mohammad holds a Bachelor's degree in Physiotherapy from a leading Saudi university; he has worked across rehabilitation centres, private clinics, and the broader AMAALA destination. The work runs through musculoskeletal rehabilitation, injury recovery, and functional training, paired with a habit of continuous self-development.

Certified in Orthopaedic Manual Therapy, Myofascial Needling Therapy, and as a Personal Trainer (ISSA), Mohammad integrates evidence-based physiotherapy techniques with fitness and mobility training to support each guest's individual goals.
