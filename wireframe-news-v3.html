Common Questions

Who can I contact for press enquiries?

Carol El Hadi, Director of Marketing, PR & Communications, leads media relations. Write to press@jayasom.com or use the smart form on the Contact page (select "Press and Media" from the dropdown).

How do I request photography or video?

Submit a short request via press@jayasom.com naming the publication, intended use, and deadline. We ship media-kit assets within two working days.

Where is Jayasom located?

Jayasom Wellness Resort sits at AMAALA, Triple Bay, on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast. The nearest international airport is Al Wajh, one hour by car.
