Description

What the session is

A bodywork session in three layers: aromatherapy, heated stone, energy work. The Jade is held at body temperature on entry and warmed by the room as the session proceeds; the oil blend is chosen at the start by the practitioner from a small palette of Arabic-inspired essences.

Across a stay, the work is small and constant. Muscle release without deep pressure draws most guests; some come for the heat that lengthens the breath, others for the quiet that scent and weighted touch leave behind.

Warm, slow, scented. Jade carries weight in the hand; strokes run long. Most guests notice the room before the practitioner begins, and the breath before the muscle release.

Outcomes

What guests notice afterwards

  • Muscle release without deep pressure
  • A breath that opens with the heat
  • A nervous system that settles into scent and weighted touch
  • Skin that holds the oil and the warmth for the rest of the day
  • A way back into the body when the day has been spent in the head

Common questions

Frequently asked

Why Jade rather than another stone?

The crystal carries weight the hand alone cannot match, and it holds heat well. Other stones in the property's range include heated salt and basalt; the practitioner chooses with the brief.

Are the oils Arabic?

Arabic-inspired. The blend draws on the Traditional Arabic Medicine cluster the property has built around: oud, frankincense, jasmine, rose, neroli among them. The blend is matched to the brief, not to a fixed protocol.

Is this the same as Gemstone Therapy or Crystal Healing?

They sit alongside each other in the property's catalogue but they are distinct. Crystal Aroma Massage is a bodywork session with heated Jade and aromatherapy. Gemstone Therapy and Crystal Healing each have their own structure.

Will the pressure be deep?

Light to medium. The Jade delivers the weight; the hand directs it. Guests who want deeper pressure usually move to Deep Tissue or Pinda Sweda within the same week.

What does the Energy Work Pillar mean here?

Energy and Sound is one of the Therapeutic Pillars at Jayasom. The pillar covers Reiki, Gemstone Therapy, Sound Healing, and Crystal Aroma Massage among others; the work is felt rather than discussed.
