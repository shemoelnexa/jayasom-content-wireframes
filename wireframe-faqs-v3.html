01

Planning your visit

You can register your interest and a Wellness Advisor will be in touch to help you plan and reserve your stay. As booking opens, you will also be able to enquire directly through the Contact page. Every stay begins with a short conversation, so the retreat or programme you choose truly fits you.
Not necessarily. You are welcome to arrive with a programme already in mind, or to begin with a wellness consultation on arrival that shapes your days around how you feel and what you hope to change. Our practitioners adjust the path as you go.
Structured retreats and programmes unfold over several nights so the work has time to settle. Shorter stays and single-day journeys are also available for those who wish to experience Jayasom before committing to a full programme.
Yes. Day journeys offer a curated sequence of treatments, movement, and nourishment, a way to step into the Jayasom experience within a single day. Availability varies by season, and a Wellness Advisor can talk you through the options.

02

Wellness and programmes

Every guest begins with a wellness consultation. Certain therapies and diagnostic-led programmes also involve a health intake so our practitioners can tailor them safely to you. If you are managing a specific condition, we ask that you share this in advance.
Retreats and programmes include a considered set of treatments, activities, and consultations built around their intention. Additional therapies from the wider treatment menu can be added to any stay, with the guidance of your practitioner.
Yes. Programmes are a starting frame rather than a fixed schedule. Following your consultation, practitioners shape each day around your goals, your energy, and how you respond, whether that is rest, recovery, movement, or deeper diagnostic work.
Jayasom brings together resident practitioners and visiting specialists across traditional healing systems and modern medicine. You can read more about the people who guide your stay on the Our Experts and Specialists page.
Where relevant, you leave with a take-home protocol, simple practices, guidance, and next steps designed to carry the work of your stay into daily life. The intention is continuity, not a single reset.

03

Stays and residences

Rooms and villas are for guests staying for a retreat or programme, with varying levels of space and privacy. Wellness residences are longer-term homes within the destination for those who wish to make wellbeing part of everyday life. Explore Rooms and Villas and Wellness Residences to compare.
The wellness residences are a distinct part of the destination, designed for longer-term living. To learn about availability and ownership, we invite you to register your interest and a member of our team will share detailed information.
Yes. Alongside individual and couples retreats, there are experiences designed for shared wellbeing, including the As a Family retreat. These balance time together with space for each person's own rhythm.
Children are welcome as part of family experiences, with activities and care shaped to their age. Some treatments and spaces are reserved for adults to preserve a sense of calm. A Wellness Advisor can help you plan a stay that works for the whole family.

04

Dining and daily life

Our approach to culinary nourishment treats food as part of the healing journey, seasonal, plant-forward, and designed to support the intention of your stay, without ever feeling like a compromise.
Yes. Please share any allergies, intolerances, or preferences when you plan your stay, and our culinary team will tailor your menus accordingly. Programme-specific nutrition is guided by your consultation.
In keeping with the destination and its wellness intention, Jayasom is an alcohol-free environment. Our bar programme centres on considered non-alcoholic pairings, tonics, and elixirs created to complement each menu.
Days move at a gentle cadence, movement or stillness in the morning, treatments and consultations through the day, and time to simply be. You are always free to follow the rhythm of the programme or to slow it down as you wish.

05

Getting here and practicalities

Jayasom sits within AMAALA, a wellness destination on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast, set among protected coastline, desert, and mountains.
AMAALA is served by its own airport with connections through regional hubs. Once your stay is confirmed, our team can arrange seamless transfers from arrival to the property so your journey feels effortless from the moment you land.
Most international guests require a visa to enter Saudi Arabia, and many nationalities are eligible for an eVisa or visa on arrival. We recommend confirming the latest requirements for your nationality ahead of travel, and our team is happy to guide you.
The Red Sea coast is welcoming across much of the year, with the cooler months offering especially comfortable conditions for time outdoors. Whatever the season, indoor and shaded spaces keep the experience gentle year-round.
Our team welcomes guests in Arabic and English, with further languages available among our practitioners and advisors. Please let us know your preferred language when planning your stay.
