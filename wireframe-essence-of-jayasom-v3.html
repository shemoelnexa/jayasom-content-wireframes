The Intention

Your First Step Into Retreat

Essence of Jayasom is designed for those who are new to Jayasom, new to wellness retreats, or who have a shorter window of time and want to use it well. A focused introductory stay – from two nights – that gives you a complete and unhurried experience of what the Jayasom approach looks and feels like in practice.

Each night follows the same considered structure: movement, a signature wellness treatment, time in the spa and dedicated recovery. The rhythm is consistent. The experience is your own. Your Wellness Advisor is with you throughout – building a picture of your needs from the moment you arrive.

Arrive curious. Leave with a clearer sense of what your well-being needs – and, if you choose to return for a longer retreat, your Wellness Advisor will already know where to begin.

How your retreat takes shape

How Your Retreat Takes Shape

Essence of Jayasom is built around a simple, repeatable structure. Each day includes movement, a chosen wellness treatment, time in the spa and dedicated recovery. Nothing is rushed. The pace is intentional.

Your opening consultation gives your Wellness Advisor a picture of where you are and what you are looking for. From there, your days are sequenced to give you the most complete introduction to the Jayasom experience possible within your stay.

Essence of Jayasom is not a condensed version of a longer retreat. It is its own experience – complete, considered and designed to give you everything you need to understand what Jayasom is.

Questions

FAQs

What is Essence of Jayasom?

Essence of Jayasom is a short introductory retreat at Jayasom AMAALA – designed for first-time guests or those with a shorter window of time. Each night follows a consistent structure of movement, therapy, spa and recovery, giving you a complete experience of the Jayasom approach in fewer nights.

Who is Essence of Jayasom for?

Essence of Jayasom is for those who are new to Jayasom, new to wellness retreats, or who want to experience the Jayasom approach before committing to a longer stay. It is also well suited to guests with limited time who still want a meaningful, well-structured retreat experience.

How long is the stay?

Essence of Jayasom is available as a 2 or 3-night stay – intentionally shorter than the Jayasom signature retreats, which begin at four nights.

What is included each night?

Each night includes a choice of wellness treatment (60 min), access to movement activities, spa and hydrothermal facilities, wellness cuisine meals and guided recovery. Your Wellness Advisor is available throughout your stay.

Can I move to a longer retreat after Essence of Jayasom?

Yes. If you choose to return for a longer retreat, your Wellness Advisor will already have a clear picture of your needs and preferences. Many guests find that Essence of Jayasom is the natural starting point for a longer relationship with Jayasom.
