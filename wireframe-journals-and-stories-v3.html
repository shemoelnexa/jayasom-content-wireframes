What we don't write

We don't write transformation arcs. We don't write before-and-afters. We don't gloss the days that didn't go to plan. The point of a journal is that the small things stack up; we'd rather show you the small things than pretend the week was a single revelation.

Editorial direction from Carol El Hadi, Director of Marketing, PR and Communications. Each journal article carries its own author byline.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Jayasom journal?

An immersive, experiential piece written from inside the days at the property. Closer to a notebook entry than a marketing article; carries an author byline and date.

Who writes the journals?

Practitioners in residence, the Wellness Advisor team, the editorial team, and occasionally a returning guest writing with the editorial team. Each piece is bylined.

How often are new journals published?

New journals land as they're ready, not on a fixed schedule. The featured long-form rotates monthly. The subscribe digest goes out at month-end.
