Family & Children Wellness

Children, parents and grandparents together through meaningful moments of discovery.

Speak with a Health & Wellness Advisor

Family & multigenerational wellness at Jayasom AMAALA brings children, parents and grandparents together through meaningful moments of discovery. Guided by movement, creativity, sensory awareness, emotional wellbeing, nourishment, rest and family rituals, we encourage growth, confidence and lifelong healthy habits.

Each offering is thoughtfully adapted to age, ability and individual preferences, supporting resilience, self-expression and a strong sense of belonging.

Nature, human connection and conscious technology balance each other, with virtual reality, interactive experiences and digital learning enriching curiosity, imagination and engagement while remaining grounded in human presence.

Wellness & spa etiquette

Age guidance and parent consent

Each experience includes recommended age guidance to support safety, comfort and meaningful participation. Parent or guardian consent is required for all guests under the age of 18, and some services may also require a health declaration, allergy information or suitability review before confirmation.

Before you visit

Signature Experiences

Find the right experience

Children's and family wellness services are designed for each stage of development, rather than adapted from adult treatments. Combine the filters to narrow by category, age, length or format.

Water Journey

The Family Hydrothermal Journey

The Family Hydrothermal Journey is a guided 70-minute outdoor ritual that invites families to unwind, reconnect and enjoy the restorative benefits of water, warmth, mineral bathing and relaxation in nature. Designed for multiple generations, it is thoughtfully adapted to create a comfortable and enjoyable experience for every guest.

Children and teens participate under appropriate adult supervision and team guidance, with individual elements tailored according to age and suitability.

Prof. Dr. Biener mineral pools

The mineral vitality pool's salts at Jayasom AMAALA have been specially curated with Prof. Dr. Biener's salt and mineral expertise. Each mineral blend is selected to create a purposeful bathing experience, using carefully calculated salts to support comfort, relaxation and a deeper connection to water-based wellbeing.

Suggested sequence · approx. 70 minutes

A guided circuit — pick and choose

It's not necessary to do everything; feel free to pick and choose. A family bookable ritual, Rasul & Scrub, anchors the journey.

Important guidance

All family hydrothermal journeys are guided or supervised by the wellness team. Participation may be modified or postponed if a child or family member is unwell, sensitive to heat, dehydrated, experiencing dizziness, has a recent illness or injury, skin irritation, breathing difficulties, cardiovascular concerns, or any condition requiring additional review. Families are encouraged to hydrate before, during and after the journey, move gradually between experiences, and take breaks whenever a child feels tired, warm, uncomfortable or overstimulated.

Grow Studios

Studios by developmental stage

These studios are arranged by four developmental stages so each child can participate in an environment that feels safe, engaging and meaningful. Each space, which has indoor and outdoor elements, supports age-appropriate play, creativity, social connection, movement, focus and calm.

The children's studios are open daily from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. No reservation is required for general access; entry is subject to availability, age suitability, studio capacity and operational requirements. Each studio welcomes a maximum of 10 children at a time, and children may enjoy independent time for up to two hours. Parent or guardian consent, waiver forms and relevant health information may be required before studio access.

Play Spaces

Room to move and connect

Play at Jayasom supports movement, confidence, creativity and social connection. Some spaces are for free play, while others may be used for structured sessions and guided family activities.

Nature, Discovery & Adventure

Outdoors, food and sustainability

Shared time outdoors invites moments of discovery with explorations of nature, food and sustainability. Designed to inspire curiosity, movement and quiet reflection, these spaces foster a deeper connection with the values and the landscape of Jayasom AMAALA.

Outside the Gates

Into the landscapes of AMAALA

Beyond the family wellness facilities, Jayasom extends into the landscapes of AMAALA and the Red Sea, where guided outdoor, marine and nature-based pursuits encourage discovery and connection with the natural world.
