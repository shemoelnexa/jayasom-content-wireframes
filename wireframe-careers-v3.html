Who we hire

Hired for depth-of-practice

We hire for depth-of-practice at Jayasom. Practitioners who keep their craft sharp across years, not headlines. Hospitality teams who hold a guest experience together without flourish. Operations and residences teams who see the building as part of the practice. Leadership who treat hiring as the most senior thing they do. Roles open across wellness, hospitality, culinary, residences, operations, leadership, and corporate functions. We hire across grades, across regions, across the long arc of someone's career.

Practitioner in session

Ethos and Culture

A Jayasom Way of Life

Wellbeing is not a luxury. It is a birthright. That belief sits at the centre of how we build teams as much as how we build retreats. Our practitioners arrive in residence for weeks at a time. Our hospitality colleagues stay long enough to know guests by name. Our operations colleagues know the property well enough to hold its standards quietly. The work is small and constant, the kind that compounds across a year.

We trust your natural desire to realign with what feels right, your own unique essence.

What we look for

Five qualities thread through every role

A genuine practice, not a credential file. Patience with detail. Calm under pressure. Curiosity about wisdom traditions and contemporary science in equal measure. And a way of treating colleagues that makes the work easier for everyone around you.

A genuine practice

Not a credential file. The work shows in how you hold a session, a kitchen, a conversation, day after day.

Patience with detail

The small things, repeated well, are what guests feel. The standards hold themselves when the team holds them.

Calm under pressure

Steady on a busy day, present on a slow one. The rhythm of a property does not bend to a single shift.

Curiosity, both ways

Wisdom traditions and contemporary science in equal measure. The third way is held by people who keep reading.

Treats colleagues well

A way of working that makes the work easier for everyone around you. The team carries the practice together.

In residence, in rhythm

Practitioners arrive for weeks. Hospitality and operations colleagues live with the property. The work asks for time, and we return it.

How it works

Application process

Step 01

Apply

Submit your application through the HR portal. A short note in your own voice carries weight.

Step 02

First conversation

A long, unhurried call with the People team and the hiring lead. We listen for the practice.

Step 03

In residence

Where appropriate, candidates spend time in residence at AMAALA. The property is part of the answer.

Step 04

Offer

A clear offer, written in plain language. Relocation, residency and start-date arrangements follow.

Common Questions

Working at Jayasom

A short index of the questions candidates ask most often.

Where is Jayasom located?

Jayasom Wellness Resort sits at AMAALA, Triple Bay, on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast. The nearest international airport is Al Wajh, one hour by car.

What kinds of roles does Jayasom hire for?

Practitioner, hospitality, culinary, operations, residences, leadership, and corporate functions. Wellness Advisors and Visiting Practitioners arrive in residence for weeks at a time. Hospitality and operations colleagues are full-time in residence at AMAALA.

Do you provide accommodation for in-residence team members?

Are there opportunities for visiting practitioners?

Yes. Visiting Practitioners join us in residence across the year, contributing to specific retreats and shared wellbeing programming. Send a short note with your specialism and availability.

How do I apply?

Open roles live in the HR system, linked above. For spontaneous applications, send a short note to careers@jayasom.com with recent work.
