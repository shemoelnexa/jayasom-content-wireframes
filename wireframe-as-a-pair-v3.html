The Intention

Two People, One Intention

As a Pair is a stay for two people who want to give their connection more than ordinary life usually allows. Come to celebrate what you have – as friends, partners, siblings, parents. Come to find your way back to each other, or to find a deeper way forward together. Come because you know the connection between you deserves more investment than ordinary life allows.

How your retreat takes shape

How Your Retreat Takes Shape

Time away from everyday demands to fortify what you have, reflect, and enjoy each other's company with renewed attention and ease. Thoughtfully designed experiences, guided conversations and shared moments in nature encourage understanding, trust and lasting closeness.

In a supportive, distraction-free setting, your Wellness Advisor helps you both explore individual needs while developing practical tools for open communication and healthy relationship patterns. Shared rituals, restorative therapies, engaging activities and memorable adventures create space for laughter, relaxation and new perspectives – leaving you with a stronger sense of one another and renewed confidence in what you are building together.

Featured Treatments

The treatments within As a Pair work across shared ritual, physical restoration, and relational practice. Each retreat is personalised – these are the experiences that run through your stay, not a fixed prescription.

Personalised Couples Wellness Counselling

Personalised Couples Wellness Counselling

60 min

A guided session supporting communication, emotional awareness and deeper connection through practical coaching tools.

Two Hearts Ritual

Two Hearts Ritual

90 min

A grounding spa ritual for two featuring firm back-of-body massage, facial Gua Sha, focused neck work and scalp massage. Enhanced by a curated sound journey to encourage relaxation, presence and meaningful connection.

Wellness Cooking Class

Wellness Cooking Class

90 min

A practical cooking experience focused on fresh ingredients, balanced preparation and simple techniques that can be continued at home. The session connects food, flavour and everyday well-being.

Couple's Yoga

Couple's Yoga

60 min

A balanced yoga practice blending postures, breathwork and gentle transitions to encourage flexibility, stability and a sense of calm while nurturing mind-body connection.

Sound Bath Meditation

Sound Bath Meditation

60 min

A restful meditative journey using layered soundscapes, therapeutic instruments, light and stillness to support deep relaxation, mental clarity and emotional ease.

Express Hammam Ritual

Express Hammam Ritual

60 min

A revitalising hammam experience combining steam, cleansing and exfoliation with nourishing products and aromatic oils. It removes impurities, softens the skin and leaves the body refreshed.
